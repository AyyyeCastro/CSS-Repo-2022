BIOGRAPHY


Hello, my name is Andrew Castro! I was born and raised in Rhode Island. However, my dad is from Guatemala. My mother was born and raised and Rhode Island as well. But, her parents (my grandparents) are from Portugal (São Miguel). Ethnically I am Portuguese, Filipino, Spanish, and "technically" Chinese. But I think that would be too distant to count at this point. As for languages- I can only fluently speak in English. However, I can understand the majority of what my family says in Portuguese, specifically their dialect. I can read and write in Korean/한글, but my vocabulary and grammar are too elementary to be considered anywhere near fluent...

I work part time as a Door Dash driver, because it's the most flexible part-time job to have, while being a full-time college student. I'm able to set my own hours, but sadly there will always be the "best" hours to work (Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast)- just due to demand. Most of my free time I spend playing video games, mostly MMORPGs (FFXIV!). I got into MMORPGs because my first PC video game I ever played was a free to play social MMORPG called Free Realms (2009-2014), by Sony Online Entertainment (now DayBreak Games). I was in a guild called Mystic Mayhem, and am still friends with many of them today.

Well... That's all for my bio page. Bye!
